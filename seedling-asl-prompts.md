# Seedling — Zee ASL Sign Prompts (Nano Banana)

Zee the teal octopus demonstrates ASL signs using her tentacles. Her two front tentacles act as hands/arms. Run each prompt separately. Keep the style string identical across every prompt.

**Note:** "I Love You" already exists as `zee/i love you.png` — not included here.

**Motion techniques used in prompts:**
- **Arrow** — a small black directional arrow drawn in the scene, like ASL dictionary notation, showing the direction of movement
- **Ghost trail** — faded ghost/afterimage copies of the tentacles in the start or mid position, with motion lines, showing the path of movement
- **Combined** — both arrow + ghost trail for signs with a clear direction AND a path
