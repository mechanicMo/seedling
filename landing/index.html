Morning transitions are one of the hardest moments for young kids. Their brains are still waking up and regulation is at its lowest.

The most effective approach is to shrink the morning decision load — lay out clothes the night before, prep a simple visual routine chart, and give a 10-minute warning before departure.

Everything you need

Up to 3 child profiles

Each child gets their own curated content selection, session timer, and activity history — tailored to their age and your preferences.
